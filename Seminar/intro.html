Planning Reading Seminar on Kasparov Theory and Stable Homotopy Theory in Operator Algebras

     This is a planning reading seminar on Kasparov Theory on Operator \(\mathrm{K}\)-theory and its relationship with other mathematical topics. I hold this page in order to get clear with the basic methods what Kasparov invented. Kasparov groups extend operator index theory and have deep links to extension theory and differential geometry. I try to figure out the original idea of Kasparov Theory and learn its relations with stable homotopy groups of \(\mathrm{C}^*\)-algebras, index of elliptic operators and \(\mathrm{C}^*\)-algebras classifications. Those are aim of this seminar.

     The schedule is a planning version, and I hope some experts can join the seminar, give reports and teach others. But it may fail so I keep everything cautious and try to make the seminar lasts to its end. Consequently I am very grateful for any helps and constructive advice.
